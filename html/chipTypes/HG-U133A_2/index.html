Chip type: HG-U133A_2

Human Genome U133A 2.0 Array

Note that, regardless of their similarities in names, all the following chip types are physically different arrays: HG-U133A (712x712 cells), HG-U133A_2 (732x732), HT_HG-U133A (744x744), HG-U133_Plus_2 (1164x1164), HT_HG-U133_Plus_PM (744x744).
